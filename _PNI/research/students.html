Student Opportunities

Student opportunities are available for research in a variety of different areas of neuropsychiatry, psychopharmacology and anomalistic psychology. This involves many options: Data analysis, Data entry, Writing of papers, Developing of Questionnaires, and Translating written questionnaires and other instruments onto computer and the internet, and involvement with editing and this web site. Unfortunately, no salaries can be offered but this may be valuable experience which under certain circumstances may be acceptable for credit at Universities. Each case must be dealt with individually. The possibility of being a co-author or cited in acknowledgements in articles is very strong. To facilitate, candidates need to be in Seattle and submit a curriculum vitae to this website at students@pni.org.
