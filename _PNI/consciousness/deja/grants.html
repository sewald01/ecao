Educational and Research Grants

The Pacific NeuropsychiatricInstitute would welcome considering any independent educational grant funding to assist with the ongoing growth of this website.  Because of the nature of this website, the choice of and content of material is quite independent of any sponsorship and rests alone with the editorial board and with the PNI itself. We also will consider relevant research proposals with either independent funding or funding for specific projects. The conditions of our disclaimer also apply, as applicable.
