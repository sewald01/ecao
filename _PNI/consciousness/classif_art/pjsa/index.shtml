PJSA Articles

The following articles have been reprinted from the PJSA. They are old and although checked for accuracy (using spelling in British English!) it is possible that reference numbers or other typographical errors have arisen. Nevertheless, as these papers reflect the pioneering work in the area of subjective anomalous experience in relation to clinical psychiatric frameworks, they may be of enormous importance to phenomenologists and researchers and they are reprinted here for academics who were unable to obtain the original issues.

  • Psychiatric Interpretations of Subjective Paranormal Perception Vernon M. Neppe
  • Multiaxial Classificatory System for Anomalous Experience Vernon M Neppe
  • Culture, Psychopathology and PSI: A Clinical Relationship Vernon M. Neppe and Michael Ewart Smith
  • Out of Body Experience: A Phenomenological Categorization Vernon M. Neppe
